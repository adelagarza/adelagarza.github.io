Amanda DeLaGarza

I am a non-traditional student, meaning I am "old" and not down for a kegger. I already have another degree and a previous "life". I'm expensively changing my path as I've had work experiences and aiming for a different direction. I am determined and I very much want to be successful and entirely happy with what I place in my life.

I think I'm pretty cool though, for an "old" person. I enjoy reading a little about everything...meaning I COULD potentially go on Jeopardy. I thoroughly enjoy learning about people. I usually take away an aspect of their personality that I want to emmulate or that I admire. I am very introverted, but most people think I'm not. It's painful for me to be "social", yet I enjoy people, go figure. I'm obsessed with MBTI. I am an INFJ, the rarest type, 1% of the population.( Take the test, life changing or your money back. It's free though. ). I try to be a "cool" mom. My kids can play XBOX and eat in their rooms, come on. Mainly, I am open for any type of coversation with them. I expect a lot from my kids, but I will gladly help guide them through the rough and difficult parts. Hindsight's 20/20 as they say. I always tell them that my job is to get their souls's back to Heaven and for them to be a successful and well-adjusted adult in the meantime.

I have a LOT of work experience. I have been in fast food, retail, banking, mortgage, admin, call centers, medical facilities, door to door knife sales (Ha! Yes. It was awful), self-employed, and a nurse. Honestly, I don't even know what I want to do. I want to enjoy my life, love and be loved, have plenty of money to not worry about money and learn about people and their lives. Where is that job listed? I'm running out of patience.
